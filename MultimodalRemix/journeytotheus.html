Journey to the US

In the early 1980s, seeking safety and a better life, Mr. Estrada made the perilous journey to the United States.

To aid him, he recruited Coyotes, a type of human smuggler specializing in helping people cross the Mexican-American border.

U.S. Customs and Border Protection

While attempting to cross the border, he was stopped by a cartel. They held him and his party hostage and demanded a heavy ransom for his release. Should the demands not be met, they would have been beaten, tortured, or worse.

Fiscalía del Estado de Jalisco

Thankfully, his family was able to gather the necessary funds, and he was released without any serious harm.

Upon arrival, he began to make a living in San Diego, doing odd jobs and barely scraping by.
