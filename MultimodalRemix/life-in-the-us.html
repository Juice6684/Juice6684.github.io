Life in the US

Life in the US was difficult, especially in the beginning as he adjusted to a new culture and language. He had to be resourceful in what he spent his time and money on.

He spent most of his time working under his brother, who owned a moving company, preforming back-breaking work on the daily.

Mr. Estrada with a woman he was dating in the 90's (Estrada Family)

In the US, he had two kids, both boys. It was a tough balancing act, keeping his work life afloat and raising his children. Being the sole breadwinner for his family, luxeries like meat and high quality food were often out of reach.

In the late 2010's he bought his first home in Oklahoma, where he repaired the run down house, and rennovated it using his carpentry and achetecture skills.

Today

Today, Mr. Estrada lives in the United States with his family. He has built a life for himself and has had to retire early in 2023 due to health reasons.

Mr. Estrada outside with his backyard animals (Estrada Family)

He mostly stays at home with his wife and two sons, his back has long since given out on him but he still enjoys spending time with his family and tending to his hobbies. He's a very kind person with a good sense of humor, and can see the best out of any scenario.
